For NewGames.ai questions, corrections, or general inquiries, use the contact address that best fits your message.

- General contact: [hello@newgames.ai](mailto:hello@newgames.ai)
- Support: [support@newgames.ai](mailto:support@newgames.ai)
- Game submissions / partnerships: [games@newgames.ai](mailto:games@newgames.ai)
- Privacy inquiries: [privacy@newgames.ai](mailto:privacy@newgames.ai)

Please include enough context for the team to understand the request. If your message is about a specific page, include the page URL and the detail you want reviewed.

Contact emails are used only for responding to inquiries.
