Puzzle games work well when they teach the player through play. The best ones usually introduce a simple rule, let you test it, then build small variations over time. That can make puzzle games satisfying for casual players, focused problem solvers, and anyone who enjoys a clear challenge.

NewGames.ai covers puzzle games with an emphasis on approachability. A puzzle game can be difficult, but it should still feel fair. Clear feedback, readable boards, thoughtful level pacing, and flexible session length all help players stay curious instead of frustrated.

## Puzzle Discovery Criteria

- Rules that become clear through examples.
- Levels or modes that build gradually.
- Visual clarity on mobile and desktop screens.
- Helpful feedback when a move works or fails.
- A satisfying loop for short or relaxed sessions.
