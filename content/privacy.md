NewGames.ai is a static website. The MVP has no user accounts, no login, no payments, and no comments.

The site does not collect account information because there are no accounts. It does not process payments because there are no paid features in this MVP. If you contact NewGames.ai by email, your message and email address are used only for responding to your inquiry.

NewGames.ai may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand basic site performance and traffic patterns. NewGames.ai may also use Google Search Console to monitor search indexing and technical visibility.

Advertising may be added in the future, but advertising is not part of the MVP. If advertising or additional analytics are added later, this privacy page should be updated before those features are enabled.

External links may lead to third-party websites with their own privacy practices. Review those external policies before using their services.
